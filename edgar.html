So here's the gist. I really like your site. There are only one or two things I see that could use improvement. Even then I'm nit-picking.

I'm guessing you chose to round the portraits corners yourselves rather than using a border-radius incase the broswer failed to load your site properly. I'd still consider rounding the borders with css to give them cleaner edges. Also, Stu's background got sliced off a little on the right side.

Add a hover state to the button. Maybe reduce the box shadow slightly and move the button down slightly to mimic the button being "pushed into the page" slighly.

You have a very fun, minimal aesthetic going for your site. I would replace this macbook mockup with a vectorized laptop. This goes for the icon at the top of the careers page as well. Just doesn't fit.
